Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

This policy explains what data the Ice Study mobile app ("Ice Study," "we," "us") collects, why, and how it's used. Ice Study is a study timer — we are committed to collecting as little data as possible.

1. Data we collect

Data stored only on your device

The following is saved locally in the app's private storage and never leaves your device:

  • Your completed study sessions (duration, mode, date).
  • Your streak, daily focus totals, and unlocked achievements.
  • Your app settings (theme, sound, haptics, daily goal).
  • Session notes you type.

Uninstalling the app or clearing data from Settings permanently removes this information.

Advertising data

Ice Study shows ads to keep the app free. We use Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • A device advertising identifier (the Apple IDFA on iOS, the Android Advertising ID on Android).
  • Approximate IP address, app version, device type, OS version, language.
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks).

This data is used by Google to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. Details: Google Ads Privacy. We do not sell your data.

Notifications

If you grant permission, we schedule a single local notification at the end of each session. These notifications are generated on your device — no notification data is sent to our servers (we don't run any).

2. Data we do not collect

  • We do not create accounts. There is no sign-up or sign-in.
  • We do not operate any backend server that receives your session data.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, microphone input, or camera input.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Your choices

  • iOS: On first launch the system asks whether you allow tracking (App Tracking Transparency). Declining limits AdMob to non-personalized ads.
  • Android: You can reset or delete your advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Notifications: Can be disabled at any time in app Settings or your OS settings.
  • Full deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

4. Children

Ice Study is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

5. Changes

We will post material changes to this policy at this URL and update the "Effective date" above.

6. Contact

Questions or requests about your data: hello@icestudy.app.
